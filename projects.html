The Hyperscooter

2022-2024

A road-legal 70 mph electric scooter.

For the fun of it! Over the past two years, Eli and I have collaborated to design and build a one-of-a-kind vehicle with truly unique dynamics.

  • 200 lb vehicle weight
  • 3x lower center of gravity compared to a motorcycle, allowing 9x faster corner transitions
  • Custom linkage fork for exceptional stability at high speeds
  • 14.5 kW peak power
  • 52-degree maximum lean angle
  • Has a VIN and plates.
